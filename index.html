LONI

Maharashtra, India, 413736


Loni also known as Loni-pravara is a town in Rahata taluka, Ahilyanagar, Maharashtra, India.

It is known for the biggest Shiv Jayanti Festival in the world.

There are many colleges in this town. Climate is dry and moderate.
