MHASOBA MANDIR

The MHASOBA MANDIR is a Hindu temple located in the village of Loni, in the district Ahilyanagar , in the state of Maharashtra, India. It is a popular pilgrimage site and attracts thousands of devotees every year. The temple is dedicated to Lord MHASOBA and is known for its beautiful architecture and serene surroundings.
