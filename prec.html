PRAVARA RURAL ENGINEERING COLLEGE, LONI

A/P - Loni, Taluka - Rahata, District - Ahilyanagar(MS) Pin - 413713 Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University Pune


Pravara Rural Engineering College (PREC), a private college in India, is affiliated to the University of Pune, India and is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. The institute provides simulating academic environment for new technology. Academic study combined with industrial graduate research projects prepare the student for professional practice in engineering.


Pravara Rural Engineering College is located at a distance of 2 km from Loni bus stand on the Ahmednagar-Sangamner state highway. It is directly approachable from Mumbai via Nasik & Sangamner or via Shirdi & Babhaleshwar and from Pune via Sangamner or Ahmednagar. Maharashtra State Transport runs frequent bus services. Belapur (Shrirampur) is the nearest railway station (22 km) on Central Railway line from where direct train services are available to Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Nagpur, Bengalore, Jammu, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Madras, Trivendrum etc. The nearest airports are Pune & Aurangabad from where daily flights are available to Mumbai & Delhi. The College is situated at a distance of about 26 km from the famous holy shrine of Saibaba at Shirdi. The climate of Loni is mild, dry and salubrious. The college campus is spread over an area of 83 acres.


The college has well-equipped laboratories, workshops, library, computer centre, auditorium, sports facilities , canteen, and other amenities. The college has a well-stocked library with a collection of over 30,000 books, 1500 back volumes of journals, 1000 CDs , 5000 e-books and 1000 online journals. The library is automated with the help of SOUL software. The college has a well-equipped computer centre with 1000 computers. The college has a well-equipped auditorium with a seating capacity of 500. The college has a well -equipped sports complex with facilities for indoor and outdoor games.
