LONTEK MANDIR

The Lontek Temple is a Hindu temple located in the village of Loni, in the district Ahilyanagar , in the state of Maharashtra, India. It is a popular tourist destination and a sacred place of worship for the local population. The temple is dedicated to Lordess Sherawali and is known for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings.
