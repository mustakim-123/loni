PRAVARA INSTITUTE OF MEDICAL SCIENCES

(Deemed to be University) A/P - Loni, Taluka - Rahata, District - Ahilyanagar(MS) Pin - 413713


Pravara Institute of Medical Sciences University was established under Section (3) of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act 1956 on 29th September, 2003 by Government of India, Ministry of Human Resource Development vide its notification No. F.9-11/2000-U-3.


Pravara Institute of Medical Sciences is located in Ahmednagar, Maharashtra, India. Its parent trust, Pravara Medical Trust, was founded by Vithalrao Vikhe Patil in 1972 while the university was founded by Balasaheb Vikhe Patil. It was granted status by the University Grants Commission in 2003 as a Deemed University, but now with the recent changes made is to be called Deemed to be university.


The deemed to be University has following constituent units under its ambit. 1. Rural Medical College 2. Rural Dental College 3. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam College of Physiotherapy 4. College of Nursing 5. Centre for Biotechnology 6. Centre for Social Medicine.


This University has the same status as that of other statutory Universities in India. The degrees, diplomas and certificates awarded by the University have approval of the Government of India, Govt. of Maharashtra and other states as well as the University Grants commission (UGC), Medical Council of India (MCI) Dental Council of India (DCI), All India Physiotherapists Association (AIPA) Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) and Nursing Council of India (INC).


The infrastructure developed at the University Campus provides an opportunity to the meritorious students to become, appropriate and needbased health providers in their own environment.


The University has a well-equipped library with a collection of over 10,000 books, 1000 journals and 1000 e-journals. The library is fully computerized with online resource of books, journals and other materials. The library has a seating capacity of 200 students and is open from 8.00 am to 8.00 pm on all working days. The library has a reprographic facility and internet facility for the students and faculty members. The library also has a digital library with e-books and e-journals.
