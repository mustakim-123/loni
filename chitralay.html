CHITRALAY THEATER

The Chitralay Theater is a popular theater in the city of Loni-pravara,Ahilyanagar, Maharashtra, India. The theater has a rich history of producing and staging numerous plays, musicals, and other performances. It has been a hub for the city's theater enthusiasts and has played a significant role in promoting the art of theater in India.
